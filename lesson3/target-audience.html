Mexico Chamber of commerce

Description Target Audience

Foreign companies who wants to coordinate with like-minded partners to further develop their businesses across the border for manufacturing, trade, and other investment opportunities. Working with Mexican labor groups to help your organization find matching legal workers with the unique skills that may be needed.

Persona 1

International Business

Foreign companies seeking markets in Mexico, offering services or products to the government and/or local companies. These companies seek technology, capital, raw materials, knowledge, and/or local labor. To do this, companies must have a firm knowledge of the economic and political context, and the possible risks of the market they intend to enter.

Persona 2

Local Business

Local companies seeking to expand their business regionally looking for foreign or local investors, potential partners, and new ways to offer their services and products. They are medium-sized companies seeking greater exposure and renown in the Mexican market.

Scenarios

  • How can I invest in Mexico from the United States?
  • Networking activities in Mexico
  • Committee and council offered in Mexico for commerce
  • Advertising programs for commerce in Mexico
